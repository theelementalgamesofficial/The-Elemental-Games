Skard

The first settlement of both Erenias, the effective capital of Northern Erenia. The home city of our heroes.

The smallest of all the modern settlements, but as the pride of Northern Erenia, also perhaps the best tolerated. Claims to produce the greatest heroes. Insignificantly small population, main language Common Erenian, with students trained in Northern, Common and Southern. The only settlement to permit mock-training of ancient weaponry. Ethnically extremely ambiguous, with populations from all over the world.

LFHAI

The antique bioweapon supposedly lost by the Musans, found by the council of Northern Erenia, shifted to Fort Erlen in Southern Erenia for further study.

Northern Erenian scientist Jack Robertson discovered it to be malicious. After extensive study by Robertson - both of the bomb and the ancient texts - he concluded that it was built to target most of humanity - all but the teenagers; he responded to the threat by hacking a failsafe into the bomb, to be activated by a code... if anyone would just realise.

The Skardians

They are not your typical children.

Taught from birth to fight, to kill, to destroy, they know nothing else. And yet... somehow, they are all the same as any other child; scared sick, worried, sobbing their way through battles that seem necessary but aren't. As a matter of fact, they're normal, scared teenagers.

They're just deadlier.

The Southerners

Ethnically ambiguous, populated with populations as varied as the Northerners. Mortal enemies of the Northerners ever since they split, they are indoctrinated - the same as the Northerners - to kill, to destroy.

And what could be more fun to destroy than trespassing Northerners?
